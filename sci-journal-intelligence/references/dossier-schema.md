## Quality Checks

- Do not mix official rules with inferred patterns.
- Do not treat publisher marketing claims as verified metrics without a source.
- Do not use a sister journal's author guidelines unless the target journal explicitly shares them.
- Do not claim SCIE/SCI status unless an authoritative source or official journal page supports it.
