# Source Ledger Template

Use this template whenever literature evidence, model-paper analysis, or citation support is being gathered.

## Contradiction And Novelty Checks

- Search for direct contradictions before recommending strong wording.
- Search for older and newer work before saying `novel`, `first`, `previously unknown`, or `has not been reported`.
- Prefer primary papers for precise mechanistic or causal support.
- Use reviews for background framing or field consensus, not as the only support for a precise experimental claim.
