## Author Requirements
- Article type: [NEED: choose based on manuscript: Regular Research Article, Breakthrough Report, or Large-Scale Biology Article are likely for original research.]
- Word limits: No single universal word limit captured in this pass; Breakthrough Reports are about 12 printed pages with 5 figures/tables and little to no supplementary figures/tables.
- Abstract: About 200 words; no citations; must state key findings without overstating conclusions.
- Sections: Title page, Abstract, Introduction, Materials and Methods, Results, Discussion, Author contributions/Acknowledgment, Supplementary Material, Funding, Conflicts of interest, Data availability statement, References, figure legends/alt text, figures, tables.
- Figures/tables: Review figures must be high enough resolution; figure legend under each figure at initial submission; title around 120 characters; short title <=40 characters; alt text required for all figures/images.
- References: First submission can use any format; prior to final acceptance use name-year in-text style and alphabetized reference list. Prefer primary research over reviews where possible.
- Supplementary materials: Should be restricted to large datasets/tables, replicates, validation of reagents/methods/genotypes, or specialist-only protocols; data and methods integral to conclusions must be in the main manuscript.
- Data/code availability: Large-scale data integral to the manuscript must be deposited in permanent public repositories before submission and publicly available at publication; accession numbers/unique identifiers/web links required.
- Ethics/reporting: Strong requirements for experimental design, statistical analysis, replicate definitions, image integrity, nomenclature, genetic material characterization, and material sharing.
- Required declarations: AI-use disclosure when relevant; author contributions; funding; conflicts; data availability; material distribution footnote/contact; ORCID for accepted manuscripts.
