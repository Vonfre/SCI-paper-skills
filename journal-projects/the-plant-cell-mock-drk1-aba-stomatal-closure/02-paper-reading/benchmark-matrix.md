## Journal Voice Model From Mock Benchmark

- Title pattern: mechanistic, noun-phrase or verb-driven; avoid vague "role of DRK1".
- Abstract logic: precise gap, direct approach, main mechanism, physiological significance.
- Introduction move: ABA/drought importance -> known OST1/SLAC1/PP2C pathway -> unresolved proteostatic control -> DRK1 hypothesis.
- Results sequence: phenotype first, then cell-specific response, then biochemical target, then pathway/genetic rescue.
- Supplement style: validation and extended datasets in supplements; decisive mechanism in main figures.
- Citation behavior: primary literature for ABA core pathway, ubiquitination/proteasome, guard-cell channels, and drought physiology.
