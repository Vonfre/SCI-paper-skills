## Reference Risks
- Unsupported claim: "first" or "new principle" cannot be used without real literature search.
- Outdated citation: ABA guard-cell pathway has classic and newer work; avoid only citing old reviews.
- Missing primary source: ABI1, OST1, SLAC1, ubiquitination assay methods, proteasome inhibitor interpretation.
- Style issue: Final The Plant Cell version should use author-date in text and alphabetized references.
