5. Main result sequence: DRK1 expression -> mutant drought/guard-cell phenotype -> DRK1-ABI1 interaction -> ABI1 ubiquitination/degradation -> OST1-SLAC1 signaling rescue -> model.
6. Interpretation: DRK1 acts as an inducible proteostatic amplifier of ABA signaling in guard cells.
7. Journal-specific significance: Links ubiquitin-mediated protein turnover to rapid environmental signaling and stomatal control.
