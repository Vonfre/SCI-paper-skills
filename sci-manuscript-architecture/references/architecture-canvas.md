## Architecture Quality Checks

- Every result subsection must answer a scientific question.
- Every major claim must have a data source or citation path.
- Figure order should teach the reader, not mirror lab chronology unless the journal's benchmark papers do so.
- The discussion should not introduce a stronger claim than the results support.
